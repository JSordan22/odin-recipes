Taco Meat Recipe

Description

Todays first recipe is Taco Meat! This is a good recipe to make because of it simplicity.
Taco Meat is mostly used for tacos howevevr; it can also be used for
Lasagna, Pasta, Taquitos, Casserole, and many more.

Heres what you need to get started:

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of celery salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 (8oz) of can tomato sauce



Fun Fact: Taco Meat originated from Mexico by Glen Bell!


Directions:

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium to high heat
  2. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until brown and crumbly (5 to 7 minutes)
  3. Season beef with onion powder, garlic salt, celery salt, and cumin
  4. Pour tomato sauce over the beef and stir to coat
  5. Personally I use a 15-ounce can of tomato sauce to make it stretch futher
  6. Simmer until thickened for about 5 minutes and Your Done!


Nutrition Facts:

  • 188 calories
  • 18.3g protein (High in Protein!)
  • 2.8g carbs
  • 11.2g fat
  • 63.2mg cholesterol
  • 620.5mg sodium
