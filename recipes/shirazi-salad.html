Shirazi Salad Recipe

Description

Todays second recipe is Shirazi Salad! This is a good recipe to make because of it's healthiness.
Shirazi Salad has a second name which is Israeli Salad but was made by Shiraz, a Southern Iranian.

Heres what you need to get started:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, both seeded and diced
  • 1 red onion diced
  • 2 small English cucumbers, both peeled and diced
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • lemon, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon of dried mint
  • 1 tablespoon of dried drill
  • 1 teaspoon of ground sumac
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste



Fun Fact: Although Shirazi is a salad, it commonnly doesnt contain any salad in its ingredents!

Directions:

  1. Mix tomatoes, red onion, cucumbers, mint, dill,sumac, salt, and pepper together in a bowl
  2. After mixing, pour both the olive oil and lemon juice into the bowl

    3. Thats right, only two steps!



Nutrition Facts:

  • 145calories
  • 2.6g protein
  • 13.8g carbs
  • 10.7g fat
  • 51.9mg sodium
