French Toast Recipe

Description

Todays third and final recipe is French Toast! This is a good recipe to make because of it's full of flavor.
French Toast can be made with breads like brioche, sourdough, french bread, challah, and many more!

Heres what you need to get started:

  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • salt to taste
  • 6 bread slices (2 for each Toast)
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter



Fun Fact: French Toast isnt actually made in France!

Directions:

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl
  2. Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side


Nutrition Facts:

  • 240 calories
  • 10.6g protein
  • 33.6g carbohydrates
  • 6.4g fat
  • 128.3mg cholesterol
  • 477.7mg sodium
